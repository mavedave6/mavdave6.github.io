The Catcher in the Rye

The Catcher in the Rye is a novel by J. D. Salinger. It tells the story of Holden Caulfield, a rebellious teenager who has been expelled from his prep school. The novel has been a frequent target of censorship and is often taught in American high schools.

To Kill a Mockingbird

To Kill a Mockingbird is a novel by Harper Lee. It is set in the 1930s in the fictional town of Maycomb, Alabama, and follows the story of a young girl named Scout Finch as she grows up and learns about racism and prejudice in the Deep South. The novel was published in 1960 and has since been translated into more than 40 languages.
