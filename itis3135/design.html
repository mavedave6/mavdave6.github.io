Project Design


Project Overview:

  • Purpose: The purpose of my application is to display book recommendations from a catalog of books I've already read. It will also provide functionality; when you click on the books, the description is displayed at the bottom of the page.
  • Users: This application targets book lovers and people like me who would like a review before getting into a book.
  • Contents: This website displays the books, their description, a newsletter sign up, and an about page.
